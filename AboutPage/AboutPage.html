All About Liam Cote

Liam Cote’s story begins with a profound connection to music fostered at an early age. At just seven years old, Liam attended his first concert with his father, a Halloween event featuring bands like Rob Zombie, Anthrax, and Judas Priest at the Long Beach Arena. This ignited a passion for music that has since become one of the defining aspects of his life.

Growing up with the sounds of Black Sabbath, System of a Down, and Slayer, thanks to his father and Uncle Will, Liam developed an unwavering love for the metal genre. Music has been a source of joy and inspiration throughout his life, with Liam attending over one hundred concerts to date.

While music is a major part of his identity, Liam’s academic pursuits are equally important. He is working towards a bachelor’s degree in psychology, driven by a desire to understand the complexities of the human mind. Despite challenges along the way, he is proud to have been accepted into UCLA.

Liam credits his parents for being his biggest inspirations, instilling in him the values of hard work and perseverance. This has influenced his approach to both personal and academic challenges.

Outside of school and music, Liam enjoys a balanced life, including playing video games, watching soccer, and exploring new concerts. He dreams of learning a new language and hopes to leave behind a legacy of positivity, remembered by his friends and family as someone who made a meaningful impact.
